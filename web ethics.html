WEB ETHICS

Web Ethics if put simply...is the right and wrong guide of what to do in the internet . Its the "online behaviour" rule book you need to follow when you are navigating the internet.. Just like any rule book, it has standards ,laws, framework, bla bla..

Its actually pretty simple..here are some few ethics...

SOME COMMON WEB ETHICS:

  1. Citing sources when using someone else's work
  2. Acknowledge someone for his/her efforts
  3. Your computer must be safe to store your data
  4. Avoiding Hacking, breaching security, all that illegal techy stuff..
  5. Share someone else's information with their notice
  6. Avoid Piracy and harassment
  7. Don't download stuff illegaly using torrents,etc.

 

WHAT ARE NETIQUETTES??

To explain it easily it can be said...

NET + ETTIQUETTES= NETIQUETTES!!

Hence it can be said, Netiquettes are just a classy way of saying "online ettiquettes" . These are the correct conduct a person must follow on the internet. So yeah, there you go.
